Crystals Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. If you click on one, you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

To win the game, you must match your score to the random number. However, if your score goes over the random number, you will lose.

The number of points each crystal adds will not be shown until you click on them.

Every time a new game starts, the points for each crystal and the random number will change.
